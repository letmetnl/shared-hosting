How was OpenTabs founded?

OpenTabs was founded in June 2019 by Umberto Greco, a Spanish & Italian 20 year old on a mission to end poverty and stop climate change in innovate ways. Born to humanitarian aid workers, Umberto grew up around the world in countries such as India, North Korea, Sudan, Malawi, Zimbabwe, Italy, U.S.A, and is currently living in Spain where he studies business administration at IE University. His diverse background coupled with his passion for entrepreneurship & technology resulted in OpenTabs, a nonprofit startup aimed at solving some of humanity’s greatest challenges, and getting everyone involved in doing so. To learn more about our journey, follow us on Instagram, YouTube, or LinkedIn.
