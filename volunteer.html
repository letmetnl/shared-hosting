    STUDENT PROGRAMS

    Are you a student in high school or university and passionate about making a difference in the fights against climate change, poverty and other pressing issues threatening our society today?
    OpenTabs’ Student Programs provides simple opportunities and ideas with high impact that will enable you and your high school or university community to become a true fosterer of change. Our programs differ in time and commitment.

    To participate sign up below!

    How can you make a difference?

    Be An Impact Multiplier

    Referring friends, family, teachers and other members of your community to the OpenTabs extension to generate more funds for donations and microloans. A 100% online program aimed at anyone looking to dedicate time towards creating a positive impact. Autogeneratable certificates are available to officially recognise the hours you’ve committed as a volunteer.

    Become A Master-Tabber

    Improving the sustainability of your school by building and leading the presence of OpenTabs. This is typically done through a mix of contacting decision-making bodies, creating and leading a club, association or society, or carrying out other initiatives advocating for the download and use of the extension by various members of your institution’s community.
